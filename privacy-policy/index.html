Privacy Policy

General

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how Vorx Inc. ("the Company," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your Personal Data. "Personal Data" refers to information, such as name, phone number, email address, and other personal identifiers, that can be associated with an identifiable individual.

We collect Personal Data in accordance with this Policy. By interacting with us or using our services, you consent to the collection of such data. This enables us to provide you with the services you have requested and to communicate effectively with you.

Purposes for Which We May Collect and Use Your Data

We may collect and use your Personal Data for the following reasons:

  • To fulfill obligations related to our provision of goods and/or services.

  • To manage our relationship with you.

  • To comply with applicable laws and regulations or assist in law enforcement and governmental investigations.
  • For other purposes for which you have provided the information.
  • To share with third-party service providers and governmental or regulatory authorities for the above-listed purposes.
  • For any other incidental business purposes related to or in connection with the above.

Disclosure of Personal Data

We may disclose your Personal Data:

  • As required to fulfill our obligations related to the provision of goods and services you have requested.
  • To third-party service providers and other organizations we engage to perform functions related to the above-listed purposes.

Even if your relationship with us has ended, the purposes listed above may continue to apply for a reasonable period.

Retention of Data

We will retain your Personal Data for as long as necessary, in secure storage facilities. We will take reasonable steps to securely destroy or permanently de-identify your Personal Data when it is no longer needed. You may also request the deletion of your Personal Data.

Subject to applicable laws, we may send marketing materials through various channels, including email and newsletters, about products or services that may interest you. You can opt-out of receiving these materials at any time by following the unsubscribe instructions in any communication.

Transfer of Data Between Jurisdictions

As a global business, we may transfer Personal Data to subsidiaries and affiliates worldwide. We will take appropriate measures to ensure your data is treated securely and in compliance with applicable law when transferred between jurisdictions.

Protection of Personal Data

We employ administrative, physical, and technical measures, such as antivirus software and encryption, to safeguard your Personal Data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its security.
